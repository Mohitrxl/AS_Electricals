Provide spare capacity in risers, plan for EV chargers, and leave conduits for future data/IoT runs. Document everything in a single-line diagram backed by BIM files.

3. Execute with phased cutovers

Break work into zones, schedule night shifts, and use temporary power panels so tenants experience negligible downtime.
